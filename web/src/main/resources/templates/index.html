Life Expectancy Analysis!

The purpose of our project is to Analyze some data using Apache Spark about the Life Expectancy at Birth by state and Census Track for the period 2010-2015. We will be presenting several statistics about the transformations we found in the sections below.

Learn more »

Statistic 1

Life Expectancy Average by State. We will compare all 50 states and analyze the differences between them, returning the most relevant finds.

Statistic 2

Life Expectancy Average by County. We will compare all counties from a specific state and analyze the differences between them, returning the most relevant finds.

View Results »

Statistic 3

Life Expectancy Greater than 80. We will compare all 50 states and analyze which states have the greatest life expectancy averages, returning the most relevant finds.
